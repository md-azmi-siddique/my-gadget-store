WIFI Router

A Wi-Fi router is a networking device that enables wireless internet connectivity within a home or office. It acts as a central hub, distributing internet signals to multiple devices like smartphones, laptops, and smart gadgets through wireless connections. Routers come with various features, including dual-band support, security protocols, and advanced settings for customizing network preferences, making them essential for modern connectivity needs.
